# Simulated portfolios (how it works)

This app uses simulated transaction data until a real brokerage integration is added.

## Connect flow
1) Log in.
2) Click **Connect Brokerage Account**.
3) Choose **Investment Portfolio 1** or **Investment Portfolio 2**.
4) The dashboard loads transactions and positions for that portfolio.

## Portfolio 1
- 500 buy/sell transactions
- Date range: January 2023 to December 2025
- Mix of US large-cap stocks and ETFs

## Portfolio 2
- 50 buy/sell transactions
- ETFs like SPY, QQQ, IVV, VUG, plus S&P 500 stocks

## What is real vs simulated
- Transactions are simulated.
- Prices are fetched live from Massive.
